Solar Atlas is a 3D simulation targeted to anyone who wants
to learn about the universe, with a focus on our home, the Solar system.

You have the opportunity to vsualize and understand how each celestial object
orbits and functions in the solar system.

What else is there to see? What else is there to learn?


Solar atlas is developed on unreal engine 5,
with all the 3D models
that are made in blender as well
as the textures, with the help of
nasa's color maps and topography maps.

The project will be expanded upon to make it
public and interactive as
an e-learning application available on steam
and will be released in Q2 2025.

Quasar is a chatbot based around astronomy.

object and phenomena.
The chatbot is built using OpenAI's API,
and runs with the gpt-3.5 model data

Ask Quasar anything, and receive an educative
and in-depth answer.

Programmed with python, with a limit rate of 20
messages per hour (as to not abuse the usage)
